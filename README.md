## Overview

This repository contains my submission for the DeepThought Selection 2026 Round 1 Business Analytics Assignment.

The objective of the assignment was to identify and evaluate potential "Federer Companies" in Pune that align with DeepThought's Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).

The selected companies were evaluated based on:

* Manufacturing capability
* Operational maturity
* Leadership quality
* Growth potential
* Market positioning
* Organizational systems

## Research Methodology

### Stage 1 – Longlist Creation

Created a longlist of approximately 50 Pune-based companies using:

* Company websites
* LinkedIn
* Industry directories
* Google Search
* IndiaMART

### Stage 2 – Qualification Filtering

Applied two mandatory filters:

#### E1 – Producer Qualification

The company must:

* Manufacture products
* Own production facilities
* Operate engineering/manufacturing infrastructure

Excluded:

* Traders
* Distributors
* Resellers
* Consultants
* Pure service organizations

#### E2 – Accessibility Qualification

The company must have:

* Active operational presence
* Manufacturing or engineering facilities
* Verifiable business operations

### Stage 3 – Federer Scoring

Companies were evaluated using:

* C3 – Differentiation
* C4 – Decision Maker Quality
* C5 – Growing Sector
* C6 – Growth Signals
* C7 – Systems Maturity
* C8 – Leadership Succession

---

## AI Usage & Hallucination Control

AI tools were used for:

* Company discovery
* Data organization
* Information summarization
* Preliminary scoring

To minimize hallucinations:

* Information was cross-verified using multiple sources
* Company websites were reviewed manually
* Leadership information was validated
* Unsupported claims were removed
* Evidence was required for scoring decisions
